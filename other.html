Nutrition Tips

Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit processed foods and sugars.

  • Aim for 5 servings of fruits/veggies daily.
  • Stay hydrated with at least 8 glasses of water.
Learn more from WHO

Exercise Guides

Regular physical activity improves heart health, mood, and energy. Aim for 150 minutes of moderate exercise weekly.

  • Try walking, cycling, or yoga.
  • Include strength training 2-3 times a week.
CDC Exercise Guidelines

Mental Health Resources

Prioritize mental well-being through mindfulness, sleep, and social connections.

  • Practice deep breathing or meditation.
  • Seek help if feeling overwhelmed.
WHO Mental Health Info

General Wellness

Combine healthy habits for overall wellness. Track progress and consult professionals.
